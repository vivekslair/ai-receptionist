## Products and Services

### 1. CCTV Surveillance Systems
Supply, installation, and maintenance of CCTV camera systems for homes, offices, shops, warehouses, factories, hospitals, and outdoor areas. Solutions include:
- HD and 4K IP cameras
- Dome cameras, bullet cameras, PTZ cameras
- Night-vision and IR cameras
- Analog (HDCVI/AHD) and IP-based NVR/DVR systems
- Remote monitoring via mobile app
- Outdoor weatherproof cameras

### 2. Biometric Access Control
Fingerprint and face-recognition based access control systems for offices, factories, and secure facilities. Products include:
- Fingerprint attendance machines
- Face recognition terminals
- RFID card-based access control
- Door access controllers with electric locks
- Time and attendance software integration

### 3. Access Control Systems
Electronic door access and gate management solutions:
- RFID card readers and proximity readers
- Electromagnetic locks and electric strikes
- Barrier gates and boom barriers
- Turnstile gates
- Intercom integration

### 4. Intruder Alarm and Burglar Alarm Systems
Detection and alerting systems to protect premises:
- Motion sensors (PIR)
- Door and window magnetic contact sensors
- Glass break detectors
- Siren and strobe alerts
- GSM dialers for SMS and call alerts
- Central monitoring integration

### 5. Home Automation
Smart home and office automation solutions:
- Lighting control (scene setting, dimming, scheduling)
- Curtain and blind motor control
- Smart switches and touch panels
- Integration with voice assistants
- Mobile app-based remote control
- Air conditioning and appliance automation

### 6. Video Door Phones and Intercoms
Video and audio intercom systems for apartments, villas, offices, and gated communities:
- Wired and wireless video door phones
- Colour video door phone panels
- Multi-apartment intercom systems
- IP video door phones with remote unlock

### 7. Fire Alarm Systems
Life safety fire detection and alarm solutions:
- Conventional and addressable fire alarm panels
- Smoke detectors, heat detectors, manual call points
- Hooters and sounders
- Integration with building management systems

### 8. Networking and Structured Cabling
IT and networking infrastructure:
- Network switches and routers (CISCO, D-Link, TP-Link)
- Structured LAN cabling (Cat5e, Cat6)
- Wi-Fi access points and wireless networking
- Server room setup and rack installation
- Passive networking components

### 9. Electric Fencing
Perimeter security using energised electric fences:
- High-security electric fence energisers
- Perimeter detection and alarm integration
- Suitable for industrial plots, farms, villas

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: Do you offer free site surveys?**
Yes, SGI Netronics provides free site surveys in and around Kochi/Ernakulam. Contact us to schedule one.

**Q: Do you install for residential customers?**
Yes, we serve both residential and commercial clients — from a single home to large housing complexes.

**Q: Can you service or maintain systems installed by other companies?**
Yes, we service and repair CCTV, alarm, and access control systems from any brand or installer.

**Q: Do you provide systems outside Kochi?**
Yes, we serve clients across Kerala and can discuss outstation projects.

**Q: What is an AMC?**
An Annual Maintenance Contract (AMC) ensures your security systems are regularly serviced and faults are fixed quickly. It gives peace of mind that your investment is protected.

**Q: Can I view my CCTV cameras on my mobile phone?**
Yes. Modern IP and HD-analog DVR/NVR systems allow remote viewing on iOS and Android devices via dedicated apps (e.g., Hik-Connect, gDMSS).

**Q: How many cameras do I need for my home?**
It depends on your property layout. A typical villa or apartment may need 4–8 cameras to cover entry points, parking, and key areas. SGI Netronics will advise during the free site survey.
